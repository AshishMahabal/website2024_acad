Aspen birds

I note here the birds that I have observed in and around Aspen, Colorado from 28 May 2001 through 8 June 2001. In noting the birds I have made use of the list of species and pictures I found at MBR. It is an excellent site for a lot of other information too.

On to the birds I have seen.
